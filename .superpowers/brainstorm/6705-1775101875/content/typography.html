Playfair Display or Lora italic for headings + Inter for everything else. Feels editorial, premium, and trustworthy — like a bank or insurance brand. Distinctive and less common in this space.

Barlow Condensed or DM Sans Extra Bold for headings — uppercase, tight, strong. Body stays clean Inter. High-impact without being aggressive. Common in automotive brands.
